São Paulo Brazil Temple

About The Temple

Description: The São Paulo Brazil Temple is the first temple built in Brazil and serves as a spiritual center for Latter-day Saints in the region.

Location: São Paulo, Brazil

Dedicated: October 30, 1978 Dedicatory Prayer

Redicated: February 22, 2004 Dedicatory Prayer
